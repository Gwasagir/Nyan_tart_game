README AND BUTTONS:
Start game by pressing space
Play game by moving your mouse.
The cat will follow the mouse.
Pressing space or mouse1 click, you will accelerate in the direction the cat is facing.
If you have superpower available, press CTRL or mouse2 to active that sht!

While having superpower (shting rainbow) activated, you will eat the tarts and gain points.
While not having superpower, the tarts will kill you and end the game.

Start new game by pressing F2.
